Our Story

Once upon a time there was a young man called Hanrich Potgieter, he worked long hard hours at a company called Buhler. This young man was always eager to learn new things, working himself up for 5 long years. One sunny morning he had an urge to gain even more knowledge, he sat down with his father telling him that tomorrow he will quit his job and enroll himself at University of Pretoria. His shocked dad told him to follow his dreams and so Hanrich Potgieter enrolled for a BSc Computer science degree to gain more knowledge.

How we met University Of Pretoria 2013

Hanrich Potgieter arrived for his first class in his quest for knowledge ready to show the world what he can do. Arriving at the class Hanrich noticed a shy girl sitting in the corner of the class all alone. The next day he walked right up the shy girl and sat right next to her introducing himself. The girl named Isabel Nel and she was enrolled for the exact same course as Hanrich.

Friends University Of Pretoria 2014

As the time passed by Isabel and Hanrich became best friends , sharing great memories and enjoying varsity life. They had great fun going to birthday parties, going to residence dinee's and even went to music concerts.

Falling in love University Of Pretoria 2015

One day something changed... the two friends realized they started falling in love... And as all good love stories Hanrich asked Isabel "unofficially" to be his girl :-) Isabel said yes and on 27 February 2015 they became Facebook official....

The question Hanrich oficially askes Isabel out April 2015

Hanrich is a very romantic person who always managed to get the right time and perfect setting for every occasion. Hanrich and Isabel went on vacation with their great friends to Ballito where one starry night as Isabel and Hanrich walked on the beach Hanrich officially asked Isabel to be his one and only and she once again said yes :-) - Making the vacation unforgettable

Having some more fun fun fun fun

Isabel and Hanrich fell deeper in love as they spend time with each other getting to know each other's dreams, passions and each other's fears. They started finding it difficult to be apart for longer periods of time ....

Graduating Isabel and Hanrich graduate April 2016

Fun was not all they had, long hours of hard work and studying towards their dreams was also a big part of their lives. April 2016 Isabel and Hanrich with all their great friends graduated from the University of Pretoria with a BSc Computer Science degree with which they will go out and start their careers

Getting Engaged Hanrich Asks the big question 7 October 2016

Hanrich had to go to Switzerland for 3 months to work and Isabel could not stand being so far away for so long and thus they decided to make an adventure of the opportunity and Isabel went to visit her beloved in the foreign country. One weekend Hanrich took Isabel to a town which looked like a picture from a fairytale book and asked her to marry him - she said yes with an overwhelmed happy smile, and so their fairytale started...
