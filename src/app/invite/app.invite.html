Our Story

Once upon a time there was a young man called Hanrich Potgieter, he worked long hard hours at a company called Bhuler. This young man was always eager to learn new things, working himself up for 5 long years. One sunny morning he had an earge to gain even more knowledge, he sat down with his father telling him that tomorrow he will quit his job and enroll homself to the University of Pretoria. His shocked dad told him to follow his dreams and so Hanrich Potgieter enrolled for a BSc Computer science degree to gain more knowlede.

How we met University Of Pretoria

Hanrich Potgieter arrived for his first class in his quest for knowledge ready to show the world what he can do. Arriving at the class Hanrich noticed a shy girl sitting in the corner of the class all alone. The next day he walked right up the shy girl and sat right next to her entroducing himself. The girl went by the name Isabel Nel and she was enrolled for the exact same course as Hanrich. These two became best friends.
