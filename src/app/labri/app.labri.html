Why L'abri ?

L'Abri Training and Adventure Camp was established by YFC (Youth For Christ) in 1987. The word L'Abri means shelter, the foundations of L'Abri has been build to give shelter to those seeking God. The very existence of L'Abri is to be a place of worship, a place of peace, where God and His people will always be welcome.

We have decided to start the foundations of our marriage - the holy covenant between 2 people God has created - on the foundations of L'Abri since it's very existence is based on - God we also want to start our marriage making God the center of it from the very start.

Activities

Swing Falls

There will be 1 trip down to Swing Falls for those who are brave enough for some adventure :-)


Please note that it is an obstacle course to get to Swing Falls - Bring along energy, and for the very brave please bring along a bathing suit, water and tekkies.

Trip to Swing Falls : Sunday 1 April 10:00 AM

Skaba

There will be 1 trip to Skaba where you can view the whole world from a distance


This trip entails a 15 min drive to the other edge of L'Abri. If you have a 4x4 you are welcome to join the drive or join someone with a 4x4 :-)

Trip to Skaba : Sonday 1 April 5:00 PM
