Accomodation @ L'abri

L'Abri Training and Adventure Camp caters for large youth groups, thus there is plenty of room for everyone.

We know that you will have to travel far to our wedding and that is why we have organized accommodation at L'Abri at NO cost to you.

*Please Note

If you would like to stay over for the wedding, please indicate so in your RSVP form.



You will have 3 options
~ Either to camp ( This includes bringing your own tents and caravans - L'Abri does have ablution blocks)
~ Or to stay in a shared room (The rooms are equipped with shared bathrooms and bunker beds)
~ Or to arrange your own accommodation at your own cost

Own accommodation

If you prefer to make use of your own accommodation of a nearby facility you are more than welcome - please indicate so on your RSVP form

Camping

If you are going to camp you will need all your own camping equipment, L'Abri does have ablution blocks. You will also have to cater for yourself for the duration of time you are staying at L'Abri.

Shared Rooms

If you are going make use of the shared rooms please bring your own linen and pillow. You will also have to cater for yourself for the duration of time you are staying at L'Abri.

Activities

For more information on L'Abri and activities that will be available for the weekend please refer to the L'Abri tab above.

Catering

On the day of the wedding

~ We will supply all the food and soft drinks for the duration of the wedding.
~ We will supply some alcoholic beverages, but if you prefer a specific alcoholic beverage, you are welcome to bring it along.
~ The morning after the wedding (1 April 2018) we will supply rusks and coffee to all the guests who stayed over.


The rest of the weekend

If you decide to come a day before the wedding or to stay a day after, you are welcome to do so, at no cost to you, but you will have to cater for yourself for that period.

Dress Code

The dress code for the wedding is Semi-formal.
Ladies - we do advise that you bring along your flat shoes - it will be a farm wedding outside with no additional flooring.
