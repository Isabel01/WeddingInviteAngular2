Accomodation @ L'abri

L'Abri is a Training and Adventure camp for large youth groups, thus there is plenty of room for everyone.

We know that you will have to travel far to our wedding and thus we have orginised you accomodation at L'Abri.

*Please Note

If you would like to stay over for the night of the wedding, please indicate so in your RSVP form (You can stay more than one night on request).

You will have 3 options - either to camp ( L'Abri does have amblution blocks) or to stay in a shared room (The rooms are equipped with shared bathrooms and bunker beds), or to arrange your own accomodation at your own cost

Camping

If you are going to camp you will need all your own camping equipment

Shared Rooms

If you are going make use of the shared rooms please bring your own linen and pillow

Catering

On the day of the wedding

~ We will supply all the food and drinks for the duration of the wedding.

~ We will suply some alchol beverages, but if you prefer a spesific alchaol beverage, you are welcome to bring it along.

~ The morning after the wedding (1 April 2018) we will supply basic cerials for breakfast to those guests that stay over.

The rest of the weekend

If you deside to come a day before the wedding or to stay a day after, you are welcome to do so, at no cost to you, but you will have to cater for yourself for that period.

Dress Code

The dress code for the wedding is Semi-formal.
Ladies - we do advise that you bring along your flat shoes - it will be a farm wedding outside with no additional flooring.
